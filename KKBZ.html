About the Song

"KKBZ" is an EDM instrumental by Im The Justice Man. This track features a powerful baseline and dynamic drum patterns that will captivate listeners. The minimalistic and retro-inspired cover art, reminiscent of Tron Legacy, complements the vibrant purple theme of the song.

About Im The Justice Man

Im The Justice Man, also known as Justice Vellacott, is a multi-talented artist who excels as a coder, construction worker, writer, and music producer. As a Christian, Justice believes in living out their faith daily and attends church every Sunday. Their musical journey involves creating melodies from scratch and experimenting with complex and simple drum patterns to find the perfect sound.

Justice works almost entirely on their phone using FL Studio Mobile and a MIDI keyboard. They are inspired by artists like Alan Walker and K931, often incorporating supersaw synths, FM synths, and square wave sounds into their music. Their creative process involves looping melodies and tweaking them until they are both catchy and impactful.

Justice's debut song, "KKBZ," is a testament to their innovative approach to music production. With a background in coding and a knack for thinking outside the box, Justice brings a unique perspective to their music, blending technology and creativity seamlessly.
